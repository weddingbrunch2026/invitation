Gifts

Gifts are not expected, so don't bring any. (Love you guys).

This event isn't traditional and we aren't either. We love the thought (honestly, truly, and deeply) but we've been together for so long, that there's nothing that we're in the market for. The effort that it takes to take part in events like this is a gift in itself. Scheduling flights, drives, coordinating outfits, pet care, child care, and lodgings. All of that is work that we don't take for granted.

If you feel so compelled to give something, consider donating to the following charities, they are some of our favorites.

Local Charities we Support:

Canine Companions

Canine Companions provides service dogs to adults, children and veterans with disabilities.

MoCo Humane

The Montgomery County Humane Society in Rockville, Maryland is an independent, 100% privately funded animal welfare organization that specializes in rescuing, caring for and adopting out cats, dogs, puppies and kittens. In addition, MCHS offers a range of community outreach programs designed to help pet owners keep their pets in their homes and out of shelters.

The Alliance of AIDS Services — Carolina

The Alliance improves the health of communities impacted by HIV/AIDS through compassionate and non-judgmental prevention, support services, and connection to care.
